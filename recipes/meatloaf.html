Meatloaf Recipe

Delicious Meatloaf Recipe

Made with ground beef, seasonings, onions and breadcrumbs. Baked in the oven delivering a delicious and tasty dish.

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Breadcrumbs
  • Eggs
  • Onions
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Fresh Parsley
  • 1 tsp Garlic Powder
  • 1 tbs Worcestershire Sauce
  • Cooked Green Bell Pepper, Chopped

Steps

  1. Soften the onion in a pan.
  2. Combine all ingredients in a bowl.
  3. Form into a loaf on a baking sheet and bake.
  4. Top with a meat loaf sauce of your choice and bake until bubbly.
